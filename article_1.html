10 Tips for Better Sleep in 2026

Quality sleep is the foundation of good health. Here's how to improve yours tonight.

1. Stick to a Schedule Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day, even at weekends.

2. Create a Sleep Sanctuary Your bedroom should be cool, dark, and quiet. Invest in blackout curtains.

3. Limit Screen Time Blue light from devices interferes with melatonin production.

4. Watch What You Consume Avoid caffeine after noon and limit alcohol.

5. Exercise Regularly Regular physical activity promotes better sleep, but not too close to bedtime.

6. Manage Stress Practice relaxation techniques before bed.
