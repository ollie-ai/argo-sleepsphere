How to Create the Perfect Sleep Environment

Your bedroom should be a sleep sanctuary.

Temperature The ideal bedroom temperature for sleep is between 16-18°C.

Darkness Even small amounts of light can disrupt sleep. Use blackout curtains.

Sound Minimise noise with earplugs or a white noise machine.

Bedding Invest in comfortable, breathable sheets and pillows.

Clutter-Free A tidy bedroom promotes mental calm.
